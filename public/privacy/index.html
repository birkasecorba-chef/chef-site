1. Overview

This privacy policy describes how Ugur Volkan Gul ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the SMS messaging service operated through the phone number +1 (336) 439-2433. This service is a personal AI assistant ("Chef") that sends notifications, reminders, and informational messages to opted-in recipients.

2. Information We Collect

When you interact with our SMS service, we may collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Message content you send to us
  • Date and time of messages
  • Opt-in and opt-out status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Send you notifications, reminders, and informational messages you have opted in to receive
  • Respond to your messages and requests
  • Process opt-in and opt-out requests
  • Maintain message logs for service continuity

4. Information Sharing

Your mobile information will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to operate the messaging service (e.g., our telecommunications provider Telnyx processes messages on our behalf).

5. Opt-In and Consent

We only send SMS messages to individuals who have directly opted in to receive them. Consent is obtained through one of the following methods:

  • Verbal consent: The phone owner verbally authorizes the assistant to send messages to their number
  • Inbound message: A recipient initiates contact by texting our number and replying START or YES to confirm opt-in

Consent is not a condition of any purchase or service.

6. Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed. You can also text HELP for assistance or contact me@volkan.dev.

7. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the type of notifications you have opted in to receive. Standard message and data rates may apply.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. Message logs are stored on secured servers with restricted access.

9. Data Retention

We retain message logs and phone numbers for as long as necessary to provide the service. If you opt out, your phone number will be marked as unsubscribed and no further messages will be sent.
